About Hetanshi

Crafting timeless henna since 2010

I'm Hetanshi — a dedicated mehndi artist with over 14 years of experience adorning brides and celebrating traditions across India. Trained in the classical Rajasthani school and influenced by Moroccan and Arabic styles, my work is instantly recognizable for its density, precision, and storytelling depth.

Every cone I prepare uses premium organic henna leaves, pure essential oils, and no chemicals — ensuring rich, dark color that lasts 2–3 weeks and is completely safe for all skin types.

How It Works

Your journey from inquiry to reveal

1

WhatsApp Me

Send me a message with your occasion date, location, and design inspiration. I respond within 2 hours.

2

Design Consultation

We discuss your style, outfit colours, and references. I sketch a custom layout just for you — no templates.

3

Application Day

I arrive with fresh organic paste and all supplies. Bridal sessions take 4–6 hrs; small designs 30–90 mins.

4

The Dark Reveal

Scrape paste after 6–8 hrs, apply lemon-sugar balm. Color deepens to rich mahogany over 48 hours.
